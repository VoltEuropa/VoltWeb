A Citizen List

We've set out to assemble a citizen list for the municipal elections. It was and still is a great experience to meet and exchange with other citizen initiatives from around Lille and all over France (a special thanks to la belle démocratie). However looking back now on our work during the last year, we've realized that good things need time and that time was too short to assemble a true citizen list in Lille.

We also crossed paths with other political groups during this time and were very humbled that EELV offered us to join their campaign LilleVerte2020 led by Stéphane Baly and have some Volt candidates on their joint list (together with Génération Écologie, Génération.s and Diem25).

For us this represents an opportunity to underline the "Europe" in "Europe Écologie Les Verts" with our ideas and interest to represent both the young and European-oriented population in Lille. It's also an opporuntity to learn how to do a campaign with other like-minded and experienced parties. And finally it is a chance for us to stay true to our idea of allowing citizens to run as candidates who believe in taking responsibility and allowing more citizen participation in politics.
