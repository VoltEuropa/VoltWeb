Programm

Just like our programm for the European election, called the Amsterdam Declaration, our proposals for Lille are a synthesis of ideas collected during citizen workshops in Lille and from exchanging with other city teams in France and abroad.

Do you want to add your proposal or develop an idea into more detail? Great! One of Volt's principals is to collaboratively work on our programm. Everyone has a voice. Everyone can add input and improve our agenda within the scope of our core values with our active members moderating the process. We would be happy to hear your proposals for Lille.
