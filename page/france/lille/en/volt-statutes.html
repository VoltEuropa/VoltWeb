Volt Statutes

Our longterm goal is to establish a European transnational party. Until this is technically possible, Volt is organised as a European organisation (currently relocating to Brussels) and national chapters. In France, Volt is organised as political party based on the Law of 1901.

Statuts

Our statuts have been written by our members and were adopted on the General Assembly in Strasbourg on October 13, 2018
