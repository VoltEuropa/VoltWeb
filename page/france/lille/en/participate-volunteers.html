Be a Volunteer

Interested in Volt but not ready to become an offical member ? You can still participate as a volunteer.

What's the difference between volunteer and member?

Volunteers can join local teams and participate in local activities. We don't mind if you are a member of another political party or can only be available from time to time. We are happy if you can join and contribute your ideas, opinion and energy to get the local Volt team moving. You can keep in the loop of what's happening on national and european level via our internal newsletter should you wish to receive it.

As a volunteer, you do not pay any membership fee which means you'll also not have access to our european communication and collaboration system. You will also not be able to participate in our official votes, since these are reserved for official members only.

What are the main activites for volunteers?

On a local level, we don't differentiate. You'll be part of the team, can attend all meetings and events you want to participate in. If you are interested in certain policy topics, you can take responsibility and work out a policy proposal - we'll show you how and help you through the process. You can help organise events, organise your own, join us contributing to other associations, help plan our campaign, how to reach people down to distributing flyers early in the morning at university or the train station (everyone gets coffee afterwards).

Many of our current members needed time to be find out whether Volt was something they wanted to commit more actively to. Some stayed and eventually became members, some also left again. We'll give everyone the time to find out what they prefer and appreciate the effort you put in along the way.

How do I become volunteer?

Volt Europa has a dedicated page for member and volunteer registration. Head over to volt.team, sign up and we'll get in touch with you shortly. You can of course also just come to one of our events or get in touch with our city members directly. Looking forward to hearing from you.
