Our Approach

Politics. By citizens. For citizens

Volt is a citizen movement. We believe that a society can only exist and flourish if it gives everyone a voice and if it tries to implement an agenda that leaves no one behind - finding a canon and common ground among all the voices loud and silent. Following this principle means more governing with citizens than governing citizens. More listening than speaking. More specific actions than administrative bureaucracy and planning. And more effort, because decisions will have to be made based on the interest of many instead of only a few. We are all volunteers for Volt sharing this idea of empowering citizens and reinventing politics to what it needs to be today: Made by citizens for citizens.

Municipal Elections 2020

To do this, our goal for the 2020 municipal elections is to assemble a citizen list and participate in the elections.

We want to find the best candidates to represent our communities, implement our ideas, be attentive to our needs and to our wishes - we do not require political clout, track record and competence, but candidates who are able to work with people, who can faciliatate and who are willing to take the responsibility that comes with potentially getting elected. Our list is open to all citizens. We don't have a pecking order - the eventual position on the list will be determined by a vote among the official members of Volt Lille based on each canidates presentation and engagement.

United in Diversity

We hope to be able to join forces with other citizen initiatives and political parties that share our ideas and values - on our list, on a joint initiative, or by sending our candidates to represent Volt within another group. If diversity is our biggest advantage, it should not work against us during campaigns and elections. We will be happy to cooperate with like-minded movements and postpone our programmatic differences until they matter. Because what matters to us is to have a true citizen representation elected.

Objectives

Volt strives to be participative. We hope for citizens to participate in our initiatives but are just as eager to contribute to associations and other groups, discussing and gathering ideas for our eventual citizen programme. We are also a progressive movement, so our goals for the most pressing issues will be ambitious and our ideas bold. Pollution, public transportation, the housing and employment situation, security - there are many topics for which we have to find solutions in the near future. Lastly, Volt is a pan-european mouvement with teams across the continent, exchanging ideas and finding best practices that can also be applied here in Lille. Whatever we come up with, we will advocate for our proposals to be adopted, argue for why they should be implemented and hope to be able to implement them ourselves.

We are all part of the Puzzle

Is our motto for these elections. Why ? Because life in Lille is often said to be complicated and overwhelming. Similar to a big puzzle. But we can start to see the bigger picture if we begin connecting pieces. If we work togehter. The more pieces we can connect - the more citizens participate - the more stable the connections become and the more we can see what needs to be done. Everyone of us is part of this puzzle, which represents our communities and our society as a whole. Everyone who cannot participate, everyone who isn't permitted to participate and everyone who does not want to participate leaves a gap. Our goal for the 2020 municipal elections is to complete this puzzle in Lille. You are part of it ! We would be happy if you could help.
